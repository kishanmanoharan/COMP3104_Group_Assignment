The course COMP 3104 devops course is a course that prepares professionals 
that are wanting to become professionals in the IT field. It is taught by 
the instructors that have much experience in these areas at georgebrown 
college. 


The main comcept in devops is the tracking a softwares or works ccomplete
history cycle in a transparent and organized fashion. When devlopers work
